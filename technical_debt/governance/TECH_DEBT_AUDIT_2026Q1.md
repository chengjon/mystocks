# Tech Debt Audit 2026Q1

## Scope
- Governance artifacts, ownership, and execution cadence.
- Spec drift and conflicting requirements.
- Debt register completeness (owner/DDL/next action).

## Cadence
- Weekly rollup: Friday EOD.
- Monthly summary: first business day of next month.

## Deliverables
- Architecture source of truth (SoT)
- Spec conflict matrix (SC-001..)
- Debt register (TD-001..)
- Execution board (T01..T10)

## Success Criteria
- 100% of debt items have owner and DDL.
- Conflicts tracked with explicit resolution status.
- Weekly rollup published with evidence links.
